18 August 2022

AIM stock performance analysis project

In this project I set up to calculate the performance of an AIM stock, Ariana Resources PLC, against its competitors, other junior gold miners. What was interesting about Ariana was that they had paid dividends, which was unusual for their space. So I came up with a graphic that would show their performance with dividends added back, something left out by share performance.

What was cool about this project, was that the data took a reasonable amount of manipulation in Excel before I created the graphic in Tableau. I had to think about the best way of including all 5 companies on the same graph, so decided rolling cumulative percentage change was the best way of doing this.

As you can see it led to some interesting results! AAU, after you added back the dividends, has achieved an annualised performance of 15.2%. Not bad!

Takeaway dataset in Bangalore dashboard

This was a brief project in preparation for a job interview. I found a free dataset online from Kaggle - Takeaway food preferences in Bangalore, India.

I uploaded the dataset to Excel and created a series of dashboards in Tableau.

The data revealed some interesting trends. The first being that women preferred to order non-meat takeaways rather than men.

Also, interesting, but not suprising was that men's favourite meal to order takeaway was "Lunch" and "Snack". Given that men generally (but not always!) tend to eat a bit more than women, this makes sense as they might look to order something substantial for lunch/ a snack, and women might be more likely to just snack on something smaller.
